Welcome To Hidden Treasures

Hidden Treasures is renowned as a leading antique supplier in Ireland/UK. Our goal is to set the standard for trading in the antiques business in Ireland, and to establish and maintain confidence between its members and the public in buying and selling. We have established relationships with numerous dealers, from all four provinces, representing the finest art, furniture, silver, jewellery, clocks, glassware, collectables, notes & coins, and more!

Company History


Originally founded in 1972 the Tannon Family have provided only the best in antiquities and expert advice. Our goal and proud tradition is to provide only the best in antique advice and history. Hidden Treasures is renowned as a leading antique supplier in Ireland/UK. We strive to set the standard for trading in the antiques business in Ireland, and to establish and maintain confidence between its members and the public in buying and selling. We have established relationships with numerous dealers, from all four provinces, representing the finest art, furniture, silver, jewellery, clocks, glassware, collectables, notes & coins, and more!
